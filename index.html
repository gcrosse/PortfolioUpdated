About Me

With over 30 years of dedicated training, I have consistently worked to enhance and refine my physical fitness. Throughout this journey, I have engaged in a variety of workouts aimed at building strength, endurance, and overall physique. Currently, I focus on maintaining my fitness through bodyweight exercises, which allow me to stay strong, agile, and well-conditioned. I devote approximately 8 hours per week to training at home, ensuring that my fitness regimen continues to evolve and challenge my body.
